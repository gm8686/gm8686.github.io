Applications

Apply to Bruin Quants.

This is our first recruitment cycle. We’re looking for students who are curious about markets and excited to build things. You don’t need prior finance experience — just interest and willingness to learn.

Sharing is optional but can help us understand context.
When you hit submit, your responses are emailed directly to the club inbox.
Planned process

How we expect the first cycle to work.

Because this is our first year, the timeline may shift — but our rough plan is:

  • Applications open in Weeks 1–2.
  • Optional short technical / fit chats.
  • Invites to join our cohort by Week 3–4.
  • Onboarding, team formation, and project kickoff.

We care more about your trajectory and curiosity than your current resume. If you’re not sure you’re “quant enough,” apply anyway and show us how you think.

Questions? Reach out to the board via email or come talk to us at a general meeting once we’re live.
